The Snake

Personality:

intelligent, wise, materialistic, calm, courageous, lively, and communicative

Snakes, according to Chinese culture, are the most enigmatic out of the 12 signs and anyone born in the year of the snake tends to be more intuitive than most people.

They are also said to be very intelligent; however, they are very quiet. Snakes are seen as great thinkers who persist to succeed and hate to fail.

Although they are very intelligent, they are also very materialistic and love to own the absolute best of everything. Since they prefer to work alone, they also get stressed very easily.

Suitable Careers:

scientist, analyst, investigator, painter, potter, jeweler, astrologer, magician, dietician, and sociologist

Since snakes are very intelligent, they tend to lean towards a more mathematical or scientific career.

They are very precise, which makes for a very good jeweler or magician because those professions take great care in the details. Their best career months will be the third, sixth and eleventh months of the Chinese Lunar year.

Relationships:

Single snakes will have many harmonious romantic encounters. Married snakes should not play with love because they are very affected by gossip from their neighbours. Therefore, they should deal with their spouses and lovers seriously to avoid suffering any consequences.

During the second and seventh Chinese lunar months, they should be careful and calm when dealing with relationship issues.

Snakes are most compatible with:

  • Dragons
  • Roosters

They are least compatible with:

  • Tigers
  • Rabbits
  • Goats
