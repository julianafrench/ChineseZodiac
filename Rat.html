The Rat

Personality:

quick-witted, resourceful, versatile, kind, smart, and lovely

With strong intuition and quick response, Rats always easily adapt to a new environment. With rich imaginations and sharp observation skills, Rats can take advantage of many opportunities. Rats are curious, so they tend to try their hands at anything; they can deal with it skillfully.

Rats are said to lack courage and good command skills, so they are not the best leaders. Rats are opportunistic and picky; they approach issues with a narrow mind.

Rats are kind, but they can be impolite to others. People of the Rat zodiac sign usually sleep late (rodents are nocturnal animals).

Suitable Careers:

administrator, director, manager, entrepreneur, broadcaster, writer, musician, stand-up comedian, politician, lawyer, researcher, and race car driver.

Rats often show good insight and sharp perspective, which enables good judgment in what they do; they can prevent unnecessary troubles. Rats are cautious and meticulous, so they tend to show a serious attitude toward their work.

Relationships:

With an active nature, Rats enjoy a flexible social circle. They are interested in attending many different kinds of social occasions. They like to make new friends, but they seldom further their relationships with others. Rats have a charming appearance, which can attract more friends.

Rats find it easy to start a new romantic relationship with someone, but they also break off relationships easily. They have trouble with infidelity.

Rats are most compatible with:

  • Dragons
  • Rabbits

They are least compatible with:

  • Horses
  • Roosters
