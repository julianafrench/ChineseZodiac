The Rooster

Personality:

observant, hardworking, confident, courageous, talent, frank and honest

Roosters are usually very confident about themselves. They love to be the center of attention and are quite popular among

the crowd. Roosters are the happiest when surrounded by others and absolutely love to be in the spotlight. They expect

others to listen to them and may get agitated if others don't. Roosters like to brag about themselves and their accomplishments

and continually seek attention. They are also very active and enjoy playing sports. They overcome sickness very quickly

Suitable Careers:

sales person, restaurant owner, athlete, teacher, waiter, journalist, and surgeon

Roosters tend to be far more motivated than any other animal in the Chinese Zodiac. They make their careers a priority in their lives.

Since rooster are multi-talented, they can achieve great success in a variety of different jobs ranging from teacher to soldier.

Relationships:

Roosters will have quite a variety of relationships this year. They will need to be careful when managing these relationships and

they shouldn't act on impulse without consideration. They must spend more time on their career which, in the long run, will help

them greatly to resolve conflicts in their relationships. They will experience relationship and friendship issues in the third,

fourth, tenth Chinese Lunar months

They are most compatible with:

  • Oxen
  • Snake

They are least compatible with:

  • Rats
  • Rabbits
  • Horses
  • Pigs
