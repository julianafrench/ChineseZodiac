The Pig

Personality:

compassionate, generous, easy-going, ambitious, earnest, responsible, and modest

Pigs have extremely great concentration. That is, when they set a goal, they will achieve it using all of their energy. Ironically, they rarely ask for help

from others, but they are always willing to give a helping hand to anyone who needs it. Unfortunately, they are easily fooled since they never suspect trickery.

Pigs tend to stay very calm in the face of trouble or confrontation. Whenever faced with problems, they handle things carefully and properly.

Suitable Careers:

entertainers, caterers, doctors, veterinarians, and interior decorators

Pigs enjoy fine fortune in their careers. When they encounter any trouble or difficulty, pigs tend to get help from others.Pigs are not afraid to try to do

what they are interested in. They have rich creativity and imagination.they should build up their own interpersonal network to help them achieve career success.

Relationships:

Pigs will not have a great year for relationship this year. Even though quite a few members of the opposite sex will come into their lives. It will be difficult

to win a lover's heart. They will have more luck and opportunity in the fifth and eleventh Chinese lunar months. Pigs will be more self-centered and sentimental this

year, so they must be careful what they say or they may lose popularity. Lovers should get along with mutual understanding or they may split ways in different directions

They are most compatible with:

  • Goats
  • Rabbits
  • Tigers

They are least compatible with:

  • Snakes
  • Monkeys
