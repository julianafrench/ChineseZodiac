The Horse

Personality:

active, energetic, kind, rational, optimistic, sentimental, and straightforward

Horses tend to be a lot more active and energetic. Since they are so animated, they can be seen at concerts, parties, sporting events, and theatre performances.

Horses have a great sense of humour and love to perform for audiences being the center of attention. They are sometimes self-centered, but that doesn't mean

they cannot be interested in other people's problems. Horses are rather cunning than intelligent which is why they can lack self confidence.

Suitable Careers:

sales representative, journalist, language instructor, translator, bartender, performer, tour operator, librarian, and pilot.

Jobs involving communicating with others tend to call out to horses. Horses like roles of leadership rather than being told what to do and how to do it.

Horses can make it into any career that doesn't involve solitude since they are extroverts and need communication and people to flatter them.

Relationships:

Horses will have very good relationships this year and will find it much easier to talk to people of the opposite sex. They will have great luck in the

first, third, fourth, eighth, and eleventh Chinese Lunar months; However, in the first month, they must be cautious as extreme happiness may cause deep sorrow.

They are most compatible with:

  • Goats
  • Tigers

They are least compatible with:

  • Rats
  • Oxen
  • Roosters
