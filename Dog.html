The Dog

Personality:

honest, amiable, kind, cautious, prudent, loyal, and sincere

Since dogs tend to be very loyal, they would do anything and everything for the person whom they think is the most important.

Unfortunately, they are not very good communicators which make it hard for them to speak about what's on their minds. This leads to leaving an impression of a

stubborn personality. They are born with a good nature and tend not to be dishonest. They are always ready to help others, though they view the world in a pessimistic way.

Suitable Careers:

police officer, scientist, counselor, interior designer, professor, politician, nurse, and judge

Dogs tend to choose a career that involves helping others in one way or another. They are seen as great employees who put heart and soul into the job.

They tend to be very easy-going and always willing to alleviate their colleagues workloads which makes them very popular within their work circle.

Relationships:

When making close friends, Dogs will take some time to gain a thorough knowledge of their friends, due to their traits of being conservative and cautious. Once they become close friends, Dogs will be faithful and maintain the relationship.

Dogs will not easily fall in love with someone. But once they do fall in love, they will maintain the relationship well and be a loyal lover. However, their emotions have ups and downs which impacts romantic relations.

They are most compatible with:

  • Rabbits

They are least compatible with:

  • Dragons
  • Goats
  • Roosters
