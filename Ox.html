The Ox

Personality:

diligent, dependable, honest, strong, and determined.

Having an honest nature, ususally Oxen have a strong patriotism for their country, have ideals and ambitions for life, and attach importance to family and work. These things reflect the traditional characteristics of conservatives.

Because they have great patience and desire to advance, Oxen achieve their goals by consistent efforts. They are not influenced by others or the environment, but persist to do things in accordance with their own ideas and capabilities. Before taking action, they will have a definite plan with detailed steps. They often enjoy great success when they plan like this.

The most disadvantageous trait in Oxen is poor communication skills. They are not good at communicating with others, and sometimes think it not worthwhile to exchange ideas with others. They are stubborn and stick to their own ways.

Suitable Careers:

agriculture, manufacturing, pharmacy, mechanics, engineering, draftsmanship, artistry, politics, real estate, interior design, painting, carpentry, or quarry work.

Oxen always work hard at everything and stick to finishing it. They possess a serious and responsible attitude toward work.

Relationships:

Oxen are not good at communicating with others, so they have less social interaction. They prefer to be alone and enjoy solitude instead of participating in group activities. They treat friends sincerely.

For romantic relationships, Oxen tend to keep a long term relationship. Frequent changes of lover make them uncomfortable. If Oxen become aware of their lesser qualities and change their cautious attitude of indifference to affection and enthusiasm, they will have very good romantic relationships.

Oxen are most compatible with:

  • Rats
  • Monkeys
  • Roosters

They are least compatible with:

  • Tigers
  • Dragons
  • Horses
  • Goats

  • Lucky Numbers:

    1, 4

  • Lucky Flowers:

    tulip, morning glory, and peach blossom

  • Lucky Colors:

    white, yellow, green

  • Years Of Birth:

    1913, 1925, 1937, 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009, 2021
