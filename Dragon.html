The Dragon

Personality:

enthusiastic, confident, intelligent, ambitious, hardworking, and straightforward

Although the dragon is the only imaginary animal in the Chinese Zodiac, it is also known as the most powerful. Dragons are

known to be quite hot headed and tend to have a sharp tongue. They are not afraid to take risks and embrace challenges. However,

dragons are known to be aggressive, but angry dragons are not open to any criticism. They strive for a smooth future.

Suitable Careers:

journalist, teacher, inventor, manager, computer analyst, lawyer, engineer, and architect

Since dragns truly love to take lots of risks and embrace challenges, they enjoy jobs that will allow them to test themselves.

That's why they enjoy more innovative jobs which require them to make something out of nothing such as a software developer.

Relationships:

Dragons often have a hard time committing permanently and moving forward in their relationships. They tend to hesitate because

they are afraid of getting hurt. However, when they do commit, their intention is for that relationship to last. They are

very warm, caring, and giving, as well as very generous to their loved ones. Often buying gifts for them.

They are most compatible with:

  • Rats
  • Tigers
  • Snakes

They are least compatible with:

  • Oxen
  • Goats
  • Dogs

    1, 6, 7

  • Lucky Colors:
