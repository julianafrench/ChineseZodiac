About the Chinese Zodiac

The Chinese animal zodiac is a repeating cycle of 12 years, with each year being represented by an animal and its reputed attributes. Traditionally these zodiac animals were used to date the years.

Your Chinese Zodiac sign corresponds to your birth year based in the Chinese lunar calendar. If you were born in January or February, check whether your birth date falls before or after Chinese New Year because this date affects your sign type.

To learn more about each of the signs, click on the 12 Signs button. If you would like to leave a comment, go to our Contact Us page.
