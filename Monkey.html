The Monkey

Personality:

Monkeys tend to be witty and intelligent, but also can be mischievous due to their cleverness and curiosity. They are practical jokers and enjoying playing

most of the time. Though they don't have bad intentions, their jokes may sometimes hurt the feelings of others unknowingly. They are fast

learners and make the most out of their opportunities. While some people love monkeys' personalities, others don't trust their slyness.

Monkeys love to take on new challenges and prefer the urban life over rural.

Suitable Careers:

accounting and banking, science, engineering, stock market trading, air traffic control, film directing, jewelry, and salesmanship.

Even though they give an illusion of mischievous pranksters, they are very hard working when they are not getting too distracted.

They have many talents which is why they are very good at adapting to all sorts of new work environments seamlessly.

Relationships:

Monkeys' relationships will be rather bland than vibrant in this year. That is the case since one's birth year is the most unlucky in tradition.

The longer they hope and look for a relationship, the longer it will take them and the harder it will be for them to find one. If it happens

naturally, they may encounter one. This will more likely happen in the seventh and twelfth months. If they seize their opportunities, they will be happy.

They are most compatible with:

  • Oxen
  • Rabbits

They are least compatible with:

  • Tigers
  • Pigs

    4 and 9

  • Lucky Flowers:

    chrysanthemum, crape-myrtle

  • Lucky Colors:

    white, blue, and gold

  • Years Of Birth:
