The Goat

Personality:

calm, gentle, creative, thoughtful, amicable, preserving, frank, and honest

Goats tend to be very gentle and have a strong sense of kind-heartedness and justice. They have very delicate thoughts and acquire professional skills

well. Although they seem gentle on the surface, they are tough on the inside and always insist on their own opinions. They have strong inner resilience and

great defensive instincts. They prefer to work in groups, but will never be the center of attention as they spend more of their time in their thoughts.

They like to spend money on fashionable things, but are not snobbish.

Suitable Careers:

pediatrician, actor, teacher, interior designer, and musician

Goats enjoy working in teams rather than by themselves. They work the most efficiently with horses. They don't have much care about status and power

and tend to be more quiet. They will never volunteer for any leadership role and won't take one upon unless they are asked to do so.

Relationships:

Goats will have relatively vibrant relationships during this year, but they might cause distractions so goats need to stay cautious to avoid

unnecessary problems. For married goats, a third person may become involved, so it is crucial that they maintain a strong relationship.

The ninth and twelfth Chinese Lunar months are the best for a goat's relationship.

They are most compatible with:

  • Rabbits
  • Horses
  • Pigs

They are least compatible with:

  • Oxen
  • Dragons
  • Snakes
  • Dogs
